Q: How do I reset my password?
A: To reset your password, go to the login page and click on "Forgot Password." Follow the instructions sent to your registered email address.

Q: Can I change my email address on my account?
A: Yes, you can update your email address by logging into your account, navigating to "Account Settings," and editing your contact information.

Q: How do I track my order?
A: After your order is shipped, you’ll receive a confirmation email with a tracking link. You can also view your tracking number from your account dashboard under "Orders."

Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and Apple Pay.

Q: What is your return policy?
A: You may return any item within 30 days of delivery as long as it is in its original condition. Return shipping is free.

Q: When will I receive my refund?
A: Refunds are typically processed within 5–7 business days after we receive your returned item.

Q: Can I cancel my order?
A: Orders can be canceled within one hour of placement by visiting your account and selecting "Cancel Order." After that, orders may already be in processing and cannot be canceled.

Q: How long does shipping take?
A: Standard shipping takes 3–5 business days. Express shipping is available and typically takes 1–2 business days.

Q: Do you ship internationally?
A: Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. Shipping fees and delivery times vary depending on the destination.

Q: I'm having issues with my device, what should I do?
A: If you're experiencing technical issues, try restarting your device or checking for software updates. If the issue persists, contact our technical support team.

Q: Can I reorder a previous purchase?
A: Yes, log into your account, go to your order history, and click "Reorder" next to the item you'd like to purchase again.

A: We’re sorry! Please contact support with your order number and a photo of the damaged item. We’ll send a replacement or issue a refund.
